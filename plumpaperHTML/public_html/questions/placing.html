Q: Can I change the colors of any pattern design?
A: We can change the colors of any of our cover designs. Just specify the colors you would like in the notes section of your order.

Q: Can a monogram be added to any cover design?
A: We can add a monogram to any of our cover designs.

Q: Can a photo be added to any cover design?
A: We can add a photo to any of our cover designs. Just upload your photo when placing your order. Please be sure your photo is a high quality image that will print clearly for your cover.

Q: Can I share my pictures of my Plum Paper Planner with you?
A: We love to see how our customers are using our products! Tag us on Instagram at @plumpaper and #plumpaper. You can always send us an email with your picture to hello@plumpaper.com. We would love to share your pictures on our site!
