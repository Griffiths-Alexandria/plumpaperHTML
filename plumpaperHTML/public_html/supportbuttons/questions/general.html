Q: What is your current processing time?
A: Our current processing time for all orders is 5-7 days. Thank you for your patience as we are working as quickly as possible during this busy new year season to create your custom order. Once your order has shipped, we will send you an email with your tracking information.

Q: How many weekly layout options do you offer?
A: We have 10 different weekly layout options to choose from. These include 4 Regular Planners, ME Planner, Monthly Planner, Teacher Planner, Large Teacher Planner, Student Planner, and Fitness Planner.

Q: What is the size of the planner?
A: The paper size is 7″ x 9″. The tabs, cover, and binding coil make it about 7.5″ x 9″. A standard 12 month planner is about .65″ thick. Our Large Teacher Planners are 8.5 x 11".

Q: Are the monthly tabs laminated?
A: Yes, the monthly tabs are laminated for extra durability.

Q. Can I make changes to the inside page layout of the planner?
A: We are NOT able to make any changes to the layout of the inside of our planners.

Q: Can I change the weekly spread to start on Sunday instead of Monday?
A: We are NOT able to change the day that the planner weekly spread starts on.

Q: How many pages are in the standard 12 month planner?
A: There are about 90 pages in the 12 month planner (180 front and back), plus 12 monthly tabs.

Q: How many notes pages are in the planner?
A: Each monthly section includes 2 single sided notes pages. There is also 1 single sided notes page at the beginning and 3 single sided notes pages at the end of the planner. Additional notes pages can be purchased to add to your planner.

Q: What is the cover made of?
A: All covers are printed on a durable synthetic material. The cover is a rigid and sturdy material that is both waterproof and tear resistant.
